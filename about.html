Our Story

Two Milestones, One Mission

1983

Meet Horbach

Horbach was founded and has grown into one of Germany's leading independent financial planning providers. Today, the network includes more than 1,000 independent consultants across 39 advisory centers in 28 cities — serving private clients, companies, and specialists with Best-Select-Beratung.

2026

Horbach Asset Guide — Origins

We launched as a dedicated pilot project: Financial planning for high-skilled migrants in Germany. Built by expats who experienced the same confusion you did — pension letters in German, insurance fine print, and no one to ask in English. We exist to fix that.
